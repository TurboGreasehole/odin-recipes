Pancit

Description

Pancit is a classic Filipino recipe. it's a quick and easy stir-fried rice noodle dish with a savory sauce, pork and vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 8.8 package Thai Vermicelli rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 pound pork chops, about 4 chops sliced into bite sized pieces
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup sliced carrots
  • 3 diced green onions
  • kosher salt and black pepper to taste
